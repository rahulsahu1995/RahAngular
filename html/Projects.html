Vehicle Tracking System ( www.vkltrack.com )

  • Introducing a system where your vehicle can go anywhere except out of sight.The Vehicle Tracking System (VTS) lets you see exactly where your cargo is whenever you need to. Vehicle Tracking System is an efficient system for tracking and monitoring of commercial and passenger vehicles.

    How does it work?

    The tracking device, which consists of a GPS unit and a CDMA module, constantly sends out information about the vehicle's position, speed, distance traveled and duration of each halt. This information can then be accessed from a central location through a website or a mobile phone.

    Benefits

    • Optimizes fuel costs with better route planning, cuts down on overtime and increases operational efficiency.
    • Real time alerts on deviation of vehicle from the predefined route schedule, over speeding, fuel thefts, temperature monitoring etc.
    • vehicle can be located on a gis map nationwide.
    • fuel sensor, to check fuel theft, contamination or usage.
    • Instant alerts when a certain compartment or a series of compartments are opened.
    • Lets you schedule service calls or deliveries based on the vehicle closest to the destination.
    • Allows you to handle larger job loads with ease.
    • Improves flexibility and lets you make on-the-spot decisions on fleet management.
    • User-friendly software, training and support.

    Features

    • GPS, CDMA a single T-SIM enabled unit.
    • Integrated two-way voice and SMS communication.
    • Effective decision making as a result of customizable reports providing transparency in operations.
    • Configurable visual hot keys for traffic jams and accidents.

JANANI-SHISHU SURAKSHA KARYAKRAM MONITORING SYSTEM

  • Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of India launched the Janani–Shishu Suraksha Karyakram (JSSK), a national initiative to make available better health facilities for women and child.

The new JSSK initiative is estimated to benefit more than one crore pregnant women & newborns who access public health institutions every year in both urban & rural areas.

    Call center application keeps track of vehicles carry the pregnant women and keeps the track of their feedback.

SPECIAL NEWBORN CARE UNIT

  • SNCU project is the joint venture of UNICEF and State Governments to protect New Born Babies and their Mothers by providing timely medical help in urban and remote areas, specially to reduce the mortality of sick newborns. Multiple SNCU units are established in a District, these units are connected to with this Web Application hence to the central database.

    This Web application is centrally deployed for 90 districts in Madhya Pradesh, Hariyana, and Maharastra.

    Application keeps following records

    • Admission of Month and New Born Babies.
    • Delivered and Discharge babies.
